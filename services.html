What We Do

Kitchen Remodeling

The kitchen is the heart of the home. We handle full renovations including demolition, layout changes, cabinet installation, countertop fitting, and lighting updates to create a functional and beautiful space.

  • Cabinet Installation & Refacing
  • Countertops (Granite, Quartz, Marble)
  • Backsplash Installation
  • Island Construction

Bath Remodeling

Upgrade your bathroom with modern designs. We specialize in walk-in showers, tub-to-shower conversions, tiling, plumbing fixture updates, and vanity installations.

  • Custom Showers & Tubs
  • Vanity & Sink Installation
  • Floor & Wall Tiling
  • Plumbing Fixture Updates

Flooring Installation

Whether you need durable tile for high-traffic areas or elegant hardwood for your living room, our team ensures level, precise, and clean installation of all flooring types.

  • Porcelain & Ceramic Tile
  • Hardwood & Laminate
  • Luxury Vinyl Plank (LVP)
  • Baseboard Installation

Painting Services

Revitalize your property with a fresh coat of paint. We provide comprehensive interior and exterior painting, including wall prep, priming, and clean-up.

  • Interior Walls & Ceilings
  • Exterior Facades
  • Trim & Door Painting
  • Drywall Repair

Wallpaper Services

Give your rooms a unique personality. We handle everything from wall preparation and priming to the precise application of patterned, textured, or vinyl wallpapers.

  • Wallpaper Removal & Surface Prep
  • Custom Wall Coverings
  • Acoustic & Fabric Panels
  • Accent Wall Design

Pavers & Outdoor Living

Extend your living space to the outdoors. Our team installs durable and stylish pavers that withstand the Florida elements while boosting your property value.

  • Driveway & Walkway Installation
  • Patio Design & Construction
  • Pool Deck Pavers
  • Paver Sealing & Maintenance

Sliding Door Fix

Don't struggle with heavy, stuck doors. We specialize in making your sliding glass doors feel brand new by replacing worn-out rollers and repairing damaged tracks.

  • Roller & Wheel Replacement
  • Track Repair & Cleaning
  • Handle & Lock Replacement
  • Weather Stripping Updates
